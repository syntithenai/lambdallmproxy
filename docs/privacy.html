Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 26, 2025

TL;DR: We only access Google Drive files that our app creates. Your usage logs are stored in YOUR Google Drive, and we never see or store your data on our servers.

1. Introduction

Lambda LLM Proxy ("we", "our", or "the app") provides a proxy service for accessing multiple Large Language Model (LLM) providers. This privacy policy explains how we access, use, store, and protect your Google user data.

2. Data We Collect

When you use our app, we collect and access:

  • Google Account Email: Used for authentication and to identify you
  • Google Account Profile Name: Displayed in the user interface
  • Google Drive Files (Limited Access): We only access files created by our app, specifically:
    • Configuration settings (stored in llmproxy-settings.json in your Drive)
    • API usage logs (stored in Google Sheets created by our app)

3. How We Use Your Data

3.1. Authentication (Email & Profile)

  • Purpose: To identify you and authorize API requests to our Lambda backend
  • Storage: Your email and profile name are stored in your browser's localStorage
  • Sharing: Not shared with any third parties

3.2. Configuration Sync (Google Drive)

  • Purpose: To sync your API keys and app settings across devices
  • What We Access: Only llmproxy-settings.json created by our app
  • Scope Used: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file (limited to app-created files only)
  • Storage Location: In YOUR Google Drive, owned and controlled by you
  • Our Access: We do NOT access any of your existing Drive files

3.3. Usage Logging (Google Sheets)

  • Purpose: To provide transparent billing and usage tracking
  • What We Log: Timestamp, LLM provider, model, tokens used, cost, Lambda execution details
  • Where It's Stored: In a Google Sheet created in YOUR Google Drive
  • Who Can Access It: Only you can access this spreadsheet
  • Our Access: We write data to the sheet but do NOT read or analyze your usage data

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1. Client-Side Storage (Your Browser)

  • Google OAuth access token (stored in localStorage)
  • User email and profile name
  • Chat history (stored in IndexedDB)
  • App settings and preferences

4.2. Your Google Drive Storage

  • Configuration settings file (llmproxy-settings.json)
  • Usage logs (Google Sheets)
  • Important: These files are in YOUR Google Drive and owned by YOU

4.3. Our Server Storage

  • What We Store: ONLY your email address (for authentication)
  • What We DO NOT Store:
    • Your API keys (stored only in your browser and Google Drive)
    • Your chat messages (stored only in your browser)
    • Your usage logs (stored in YOUR Google Sheets)
    • Any Google Drive file contents

5. Data Sharing and Transfers

5.1. Third-Party LLM Providers

When you use our service, your chat messages are sent to third-party LLM providers (OpenAI, Groq, Anthropic, Google, etc.) according to your configured API keys. Please review each provider's privacy policy:

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Groq Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

5.2. What We DO NOT Do

  • ❌ We do NOT sell your data to advertisers or data brokers
  • ❌ We do NOT use your data for advertising or retargeting
  • ❌ We do NOT train AI models on your data
  • ❌ We do NOT transfer your Google Drive data to third parties
  • ❌ We do NOT read your Google Drive files (we only write usage logs)

6. Google API Services User Data Policy Compliance

Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6.1. Limited Use Requirements

  • We only use Google Drive data to sync your app settings and log your usage
  • We do NOT transfer Google user data to third parties (except as required for app functionality)
  • We do NOT use Google user data for serving ads
  • We do NOT use Google user data for determining credit-worthiness

6.2. Minimal Scope Request

We use https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file which is the MOST RESTRICTIVE scope possible. This scope:

  • ✅ Only allows access to files created by our app
  • ✅ Does NOT allow access to your existing Drive files
  • ✅ Does NOT allow access to files from other apps

7. Data Retention

  • Browser Data: Persists until you clear browser data or log out
  • Google Drive Files: Persist in YOUR Drive until YOU delete them
  • Server Data: Email stored indefinitely for authentication (you can request deletion)

8. Your Rights and Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Revoke Access: You can revoke our app's access to your Google account at any time via Google Account Permissions
  • Delete Data: You can delete the settings file and usage logs from your Google Drive at any time
  • Export Data: All your data is in standard formats (JSON, Google Sheets) that you can export
  • Request Deletion: Contact us to delete your email from our server

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
