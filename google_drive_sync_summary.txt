Enable Sync:
1. Open Settings (⚙️ icon)
2. Go to "Provider" tab
3. At top: Check "💾 Save Credentials to Google Drive"
4. Read warning dialog carefully
5. Click "I Understand, Enable Sync"
6. Done! Settings auto-sync from now on

Load Settings:
1. Open Settings
2. Click "📥 Load" button (appears when settings empty)
3. Settings loaded from Google Drive
4. Providers ready to use

Clear Settings:
1. Open Settings
2. Click "🗑️ Clear" button
3. Confirm action
4. All settings removed
5. Can reload from Google Drive if needed
